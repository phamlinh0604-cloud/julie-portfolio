# Cash Loan Program for Drivers and Small Restaurant Owners

## Project Overview

A fully digital cash loan program designed to serve Grab drivers and small restaurant owners, providing accessible financial services to communities traditionally underserved by banks.

## The Challenge

Traditional financial institutions often exclude drivers and small merchants due to:
- Lack of traditional credit history
- Complex application processes requiring physical presence
- Long approval times (weeks to months)
- High collateral requirements
- Limited understanding of gig economy income patterns

## My Role

**Product Manager** - Led cross-functional team from concept to launch

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Product strategy and roadmap development
- User research and persona development
- Cross-functional team coordination (Engineering, Design, Risk, Operations)
- Stakeholder management
- Launch and go-to-market strategy

## Solution

### Digital-First Approach
Developed a fully digital loan application process that:
- Eliminates need for physical branch visits
- Uses alternative data for credit assessment
- Provides instant pre-qualification
- Offers same-day approvals for qualified applicants

### Key Features
1. **Smart Application Process**
   - Mobile-first design
   - Pre-filled data from Grab platform
   - Document upload via smartphone camera
   - Progress saving for incomplete applications

2. **Alternative Credit Scoring**
   - Leveraged Grab driving/merchant data
   - Transaction history analysis
   - Behavioral patterns
   - Income stability indicators

3. **Fast Approval System**
   - Automated risk assessment
   - Real-time verification
   - Instant decisioning for low-risk applicants
   - Manual review queue for edge cases

4. **Flexible Repayment**
   - Auto-deduction from earnings
   - Flexible payment schedules
   - Grace periods for slow seasons
   - Early repayment options

### What Worked Well
1. **User-Centric Design:** Extensive user research led to features that truly addressed user pain points

1. **Risk Management:** Balanced financial inclusion with responsible lending
2. **Regulatory Compliance:** Navigated complex financial regulations across markets
